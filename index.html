About

I have worked for METER Group Inc, USA for over 10 years. I started with METER in 2007 as a part-time marketing intern while working on my bachelors at Washington State University (WSU). After earning my degree at WSU I returned to farming with my family for a brief period before returing to work for METER Group. Since returing to work with METER I have worn many hats including: Manufacturing Assembler, Market Research Intern, Technical Sales Specialist, Product Owner, and Client Success Manager. Through all of these rolls the thing I have enjoyed the most is the opportunity to continue learning new skills. METER has a diverse group of skilled scientists, programmers, engineers, assemblers, and dreamers. The programming team at METER is who inspired me to pursue a skill set in web development.
